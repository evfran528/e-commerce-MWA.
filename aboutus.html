ABOUT 404 GARMENTS

Lost in Code. Found in Style.

At 404 Garments, we redefine what "404 Not Found" means. Inspired by the iconic coding error and created by a team of four, our brand represents discovering your own identity through streetwear. Every piece is designed to blend urban culture, confidence, and individuality, empowering you to stand out instead of blending in. OUR STORY

Lost in the Ordinary. Found in 404.

404 Garments was born from the iconic "404 Not Found" error in coding—a symbol recognized by every developer. While it usually means something can't be found, we give it a new meaning: finding your own identity through streetwear. Inspired by our team of four creators, the name 404 represents both our passion for technology and our vision of fashion without limits.

Every collection is designed to combine clean aesthetics, urban culture, and confidence. More than clothing, 404 Garments is a statement for those who refuse to blend in. We believe streetwear is a way to express who you are, stand out from the crowd, and wear your individuality with pride.

Whether you're exploring the city, creating your next project, or chasing your ambitions, 404 Garments is made for those who aren't afraid to be different. Because sometimes, the best things are found where others see nothing. < /p>
